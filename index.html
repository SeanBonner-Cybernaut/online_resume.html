About Me

I have always had a passion for security. Beginning with my role as a Loss Prevention Agent for Banana Republic, I learned the importance that proper security procedures and auditing can play in ensuring profitability. When I transitioned into my new role as a Traffic Enforcement Agent with the NYPD, I was fortunate enough to be placed on special assignment, where I assisted in the rollout of new summons enforcement software, equipment maintenance, cloud migration, computer reimagining, and acted as liaison between my office and the NYPD’s Information and Technology Bureau. Outside of work, I enjoy reading, weight training, spending time with my family, and playing guitar.
